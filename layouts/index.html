What to expect

From first call to final report.

Call or book online
We'll get the pre-inspection agreement signed and get you on the schedule. Most inspections booked within the week.

The inspection
1.5 to 4 hours on site depending on the size and age of the home. Exterior first, then interior, working from the basement up through every accessible system.
3
The walkthrough
After the inspection, we walk you through findings on site. We'll also show you where your shut-offs are, where the filters go, and how your major systems work.

Your report
Detailed written report with photos delivered within 24-48 hours. Wide-angle shots so you can see exactly where each finding is located. Follow-up questions welcome anytime by phone or video call.
